About me

Hello! I am a materials scientist, with more than 10 years of experience in research, development and bringing exciting products to market. I specialize in soft matter mechanics and photopolymer formulation for 3D printing. Currently, I work with a team of engineers at Formlabs to optimize all of our photopolymer materials on all of our SLA printers. Outside of work, I love to tinker anywhere from 3D printing complex parts to combining my hobbies of photography and microelectronics.
