Backpacking trip to Sarpass, Himalayas

This is where my love and respect of mountains began. This was my first hike ever at the age of 19, lasting 11 days to climb this 14,000 feet pass. One of the most beautiful, impulsive and amazing experience of my life. This hike includes a walk through frozen lake on top, and then sliding down a couple of 100 feet! Thanks to Youth Hostel of India (YHAI) for organizing this trip.
