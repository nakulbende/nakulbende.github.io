IN PROGRESS: Cut throat kitchen database

  • Category : Science
  • Project date : 2020-2022

Visualizing winning strategies in cut throat kitchen

During the pandemic, my wife and myself started watching Alton Brown's kitchen game show - cut throat kitchen . We kept a record of all contestents, their self disclosed gender and sometimes ethnicity. We crunched the data to find if there are trends in how they spend their money, and if there is a winning strategy (buying more sabotages, what is optimal amount to spend etc.). This is a long project, and we have made the database structure, and now are analyzing the data.
