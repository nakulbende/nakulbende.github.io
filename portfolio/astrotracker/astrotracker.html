OpenAstroTracker

  • Category : DIY
  • Project date : Summer 2022
  • Project URL : https://openastrotech.com/

Open source star tracker

This build was a part of Formlabs Hackathon 2022. I replicated the wonderful project made by the openastrotech community - but partially 3d printed on Rigid 10K and Carbon-fiber filled Nylon 11 . The project combines a equatorial mount and 'guiding' image sensor to achieve a closed loop tracking of any object in the sky. Possibly the most thorough and fun project I have replicated from open source community - kudos to them. Photos to come soon.
