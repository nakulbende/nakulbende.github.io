Inspiring the future generation

STEAM outreach has been an integral part of my life. I started participating and later on lead polymer outreach through UMass Amherst by visiting schools, colleges and Boston Science Museum. Over the years, I have established STEAM outrteach programs at both the companies I have worked in, made educational videos for Boston TV network during pandemic. Lately, I have been involved in a program with Boston Public School - FormEd.

My own family came from humble beginnings and education was the ONLY way of breaking the circle of poverty - this is my small effort to give back to the world. Speaking of outreach - remembering Gregory Dabkowski who inspired my to inspire others.
