3D printed Hogwarts

  • Category: DIY
  • Project date: Summer 2022
  • Project URL: 3D Model by Joshua Neil Arthur

Floo network, remember to speak clearly!

3D printed Hogwarts by slicing a highly detailed model from Joshua Neil Arthur into 6 individual pieces, and gluing them together. Printed on Form 3L in Rigid 10K, and installed in a fireplace in my home. I wish I had time/ skills for painting this - but it looks very cool!
