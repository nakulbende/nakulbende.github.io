During my graduate career, I led my department's outreach activities. It has been a great emotional support, and looking at somebody getting excited at simple every day science examples is what gets me going in the lab everyday. For more information on what we do, please visit the PSE Outreach page.

Currently, I am initiating (and leading) a STEM outreach program: Building blocks at PolyOne catering to schools in Cleveland area.
